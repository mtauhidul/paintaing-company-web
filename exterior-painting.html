Professional Exterior Painting In Los Angeles

Final Touch Solutions LLC. is the Los Angeles company to call when you need exterior painting, or any type of painting for that matter. We've been around for over 20 years now, making customer satisfaction a priority.

Your house painting job isn't just a matter of personal preference. In many cases, it's also mandated by details like HOA guidelines, and we can cater to both.

We're not just another experienced painting company in the Southern California area, we're a local leading service provider. Don't settle for anything less than the best when it comes to your exterior painting.

While exterior painting, or even interior painting for that matter, are not tasks you should do yourself, you should still be familiar with the process involved. There's nothing wrong with wanting to improve your South Bay home, however you should leave it to the professionals.

  • The walls will need to be pressure washed. Before painting, you'll need to start with a clean surface. Otherwise, your painting will trap dirt and make it look like blemishes, as well as fail to adhere to the surface properly.
  • This also makes it easier to find damaged areas, which is the next thing to take care of. The next step is repairing damaged surfaces. This improves the quality of the surface but also means you won't have to deal with it at a later date.
  • Your painters will then remove old, loose paint, and caulk trim. After that, it's about protecting doors, windows, and lights, plus other immovable outdoor details like shrubbery. All of this preparation is why this job is much more intensive than most people realize.
  • Finally, the exterior painting begins followed by trim painting and touchups. Once completed, it's about cleanup work. Don't hire a self-proclaimed professional painter that skimps when it comes to any of these steps. Get it all written up and included in the quote.

Get ready to change your home or business. For optimal results for your exterior painting job in Los Angeles, give our team a call.
