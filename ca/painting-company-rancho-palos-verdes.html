Skilled House Painting Service for Our Customers

One of the most common reasons Rancho Palos Verdes locals hire us is for our skill when it comes to exterior painting. You can trust us with a job this important and know we're dedicated to providing optimal results.

We also offer important free consultation work because picking the perfect color is so important. This project is of a large enough magnitude that you don't want to have to do is again, anytime soon.

  • Choose colors that will work well with the type of architecture you have. It's better to be subtle and go bold with trim because trim is at least easier and less expensive to repaint, should you grow tired of the color.
  • Keep in mind, any exposed material you're leaving alone, counts as a color. For instance, if you have brick or natural stone that you're leaving as is, that's one of your house colors. So you'll only want to pick one or two more colors for the exterior. That only changes if you're painting over it.
  • Choose colors that work cohesively with the surroundings. This doesn't just mean your landscaping, it means the neighboring homes and even the local environment.
