The Importance of Picking the Right Interior Painter

Before the interior painting project for your San Pedro home can begin, you need to find the right professional to take care of the job. We've provided some tips to help you get through that process:

  • It's still a good idea to meet with a minimum of three options for contractors. These meetings should take place at your home so they can accurately assess the scope of the job by seeing the home, in person.
  • Get it all in writing. It's easy to assume what should be included yet a trick some less-than-scrupulous painters use is to offer a low quote and then nickel and dime you once hired with the details like prep and clean-up. The lower the quote, the more questions you should have about what's included and the so-called pros credentials.
  • If you discuss anything outside the standard paint job, get that in writing. It's not just about keeping the contractor honest, it' that he or she may forget. The last thing you need is to hire someone and have them completely leave out the custom details you need for your job.

It doesn't have to be difficult to find the right painter, but it does require some effort on your part. Don't settle for less than the best when you can call us for the finest workmanship and pricing from a San Pedro painting company.
