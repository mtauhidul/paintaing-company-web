Play it Safe With HOA Painting

It's fairly common if you reside in the Hermosa Beach area, or anywhere in the Southern California area, to live in a community with a Homeowners Association. There's certainly more pros than cons when it comes to living in a neighborhood that's governed by this type of organization.

However, it's important to understand that HOA painting is a thing because it's not entirely up to you how this process is going to play out.

  • Some HOAs are stricter than others. Assume you'll need approval for everything from the painting contractor to the paint color you pick. You'll have to fill out an application for prior approval.
  • Working with a painter that lacks experience working with HOA guidelines could end up costing you a great deal of money. Aside from the potential for fines, you're likely going to have to repaint you home almost immediately and that's an expense you won't want to take on because an untrained and inexperienced painter didn't properly color match to the submitted or required color.
  • Keep all of your documentation. This will protect you in case the HOA makes a mistake and sends a notice that your work wasn't approved.
